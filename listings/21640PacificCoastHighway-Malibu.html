A private architectural gem of studied informality, situated on 40 feet of sandy La Costa Beach in Malibu. The original architectural design sheathing the house in cedar shingles and dark stained wood, lends a true organic modern aesthetic and softening the geometry of the structure while blending it with nature. Views of the ocean immediately greet visitors in the double height foyer, accented by full height glass and a dramatic spiral staircase leading to the 3 bedrooms and 2 baths upstairs. The entry flows seamlessly into the living areas, which lead directly out the beach deck. All perfectly situated to take in dazzling Malibu sunsets. An ample driveway with offstreet parking and a 2 car garage complete this special offering.
