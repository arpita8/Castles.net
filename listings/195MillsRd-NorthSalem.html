Wishakan is a beautiful hilltop plateau and the perfect setting for this wonderful country compound on 17 acres. At the core of the home is a mid century style house with floor to ceiling windows that take in 180 degree views of The Titicus Reservoir and foothills of the Appalachian mountains. Expanded and then renovated the architecture skillfully integrates traditional shingle style elements with the original design. A large great room with a 12 foot ceiling is framed by built-in library bookshelves and centered on a massive fireplace. Separate master, guest and family bedroom suites occupy their own wings. An extensive exercise complex includes a gymnasium, lap pool, sauna and full bath. A separate two bedroom gatehouse is at the head of the driveway that winds its way past a barn and tennis court to arrive at the main house and fabulous free form pool and cabana. Just minutes from Metro North and one hour from the city this is the perfect weekend or full time retreat.
